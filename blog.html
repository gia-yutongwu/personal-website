Gia's very first blog

December 26, 2015 | Category: Personal

One of my friends suggested me to open a movie blog. Yes, I've watched more than 500 movies and I used to record them in both Douban and IMDB. I said I will think about it and here the blog is. However, this is not the only reason why I want to open my personal blog. I would like to record all my thoughts in this little blog: My ideas of love and marriage, my love for Math and languages, my interest in cultural differences and of course, movies and books.

In my first blog, I would like to introduce myself. I'm a Chinese girl. I'm from Nanjing, Jiangsu. I've been in Canada for more than one year. I'd say I've experienced great cultural difference. Certainly, I've learned to think and talk like an English speaker. Meanwhile, I value my Chinese origin increasingly. When I'm free, I like to write Chinese and read ancient Chinese poems to gasp what I'm gradually losing. Sounds depressing right? This is what I feel right now.

I'm enjoying my time in Canada. It's a friendly country full of nice people. I had my first work term in Toronto and study terms in Waterloo. I've visited Ottawa, Montreal and Quebec City before. I hope to visit Vancouver or work there in the near future.
