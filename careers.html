Sudoku Club Founder + President

Nanjing Foreign Language School, Nanjing, Jiangsu, CN

Sep 2013 - Jun 2014

I founded Sudoku Club in my high school, Nanjing Foreign Language School. I am a long-term Sudoku lover with interest in discovering and solving any type of Sudoku problem. I was president for the first year, during which I held many activities like Monthly Sudoku and Sudoku Competition. I wrote articles for my club and they were published on the school newspaper. At the end of the school year, my club was elected one of the five-star clubs on campus.

Monthly Sudoku is an essential activity for Sudoku Club. Every month, we attached a file folder with answer sheets on our poster. Students passing by could take a sheet, solve the problem and hand it back to us to win a prize. It was a huge success. All sheets were taken and nearly half of them were given back. This activity has been a tradition for Sudoku Club ever since.
